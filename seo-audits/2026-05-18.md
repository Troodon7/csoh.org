## Warnings (1)

1. **Generic alt="Preview" persists on threat-research.html** - 81 resource-preview thumbnails carry the uninformative `alt="Preview"` attribute. The identical pattern was corrected on resources.html in a prior sprint but was not extended to threat-research.html. All 81 affected `<img>` tags have `loading="lazy"` and `decoding="async"` — only the alt text needs updating.
   - **Fix**: Replace `alt="Preview"` with a descriptive string matching the resource title, following the pattern now used in resources.html.

4. **Static datePublished/dateModified on pillar Article JSON-LD** - pillar pages continue to carry 2026-04-29/2026-04-30 dates. Update `dateModified` whenever content is meaningfully revised so freshness signals are accurate.
