## What landed in this audit pass

This was a deep-dive strategic pass (not the usual structural re-verification). The prior audits had locked down structural quality on the top-level 36 pages; this pass expanded scope to the full site and tackled opportunities the structural checklist couldn't surface.

**Crawlability / indexation**
- Added `cloud-deployment.html` to `sitemap.xml` (was in nav but unindexed)
- Created `/llms.txt` (curated entry points for AI crawlers; complements robots.txt allowlist)

**On-page metadata**
- 10 breach pages: stripped year duplication in `<title>` (e.g. "Capital One 2019 (2019) …" → "Capital One 2019 …")
- 7 portfolio pages: titles normalized to `"<task> - CSOH Portfolio Project"` (all ≤65 chars)
- 3 portfolio meta descriptions trimmed to <165 chars
- Resource count refreshed in stat strip / meta / JSON-LD: `200+` → `240+`
- Glossary count refreshed everywhere: `230+ terms` → `300+ terms`
- Meeting count refreshed in OG title: `91 Weekly` → `94 Weekly`

**Schema markup**
- 10 breach pages: `"@type": "Article"` → `"@type": "NewsArticle"`
- Glossary: wrapped 301 terms as `DefinedTerm` entries inside the existing `DefinedTermSet`
- `cloud-security-reading-list.html`: added `ItemList` of 6 section anchors
- `vendor-landscape.html`: added `ItemList` of 28 tool categories

**Internal linking / topical authority**
- "Related defense topics" link blocks added to all 10 breach pages (3–5 contextual topic links each → ~40 new contextual signals flowing into pillar pages)
- `breach-timeline.html` intro references the Kevin Mitnick tribute (was orphaned with 4 inbound links)
- `breaches/mitnick-novell.html` Kevin Mitnick link converted from external-style (`target=_blank` + absolute URL) to relative internal
- `what-is-cloud-security.html` pillar: added in-body deep-dive links to `shared-responsibility-model`, `cspm-vs-cnapp`, `landing-zones`
- 52 of 94 meeting recaps received 1–3 contextual topic-page links via the new `tools/inject_meeting_topic_links.py` (99 inserts total)

**OG / social cards (Open Graph)**
- 10 breach pages: per-page 1200×630 cards under `img/og/breaches/` (was `banner.png` fallback)
- 7 portfolio pages: per-page cards under `img/og/portfolio/`
- 94 meeting recaps: per-meeting cards under `img/og/meetings/` (date-stamped, with topic-summary subtitle)
- Regenerated `glossary.jpg` (now shows "300+ terms") and `meetings.jpg` (now shows "94 Weekly")
- Total per-page OG cards on the site: ~140 (was 26 prior to this pass)

**Heading hierarchy**
- `<h4 class="mega-heading">` → `<span class="mega-heading">` across 177 pages (nav category labels no longer come before page `<h1>` in source order); added `display: block` to `.mega-heading` and regenerated SRI integrity hashes for the touched `style.css`

**UI bug fix surfaced during verification**
- Mega-menu (Learn / Community) was overflowing the left edge of the viewport (`left: 50%; transform: translateX(-50%)` on a 680px-wide menu under a 52px-wide toggle near the left of the nav). Switched to left-edge alignment (`left: 0; transform: none`).

**Homepage**
- 12 cards on the homepage now carry 1200×630 preview thumbnails (4 "Start here" + 8 "Browse by topic") sourced from existing OG cards
- Stat strip + meta + JSON-LD numbers refreshed (240+ resources)

**Tooling added**
- `tools/generate_meeting_og_images.py` - per-meeting OG generator
- `tools/inject_meeting_topic_links.py` - contextual topic-link injector
- `tools/generate_og_images.py` PAGES list extended (breach + portfolio entries) and now creates parent dirs for subdir output paths
