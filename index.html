What is CSOH?

Cloud Security Office Hours (CSOH) is a weekly community gathering for cloud security professionals that started in February 2023. We have grown to over 2000 members! Every Friday at 7:00 AM PT / 10 AM ET, we meet on Zoom to discuss cloud security topics, share knowledge, and learn from each other. It's a great place to network and share thoughts about the current security landscape. CSOH is completely free to join, with no paid marketing efforts.

🚀 100% Community-Driven

This website and all its resources are completely community-driven. Want to add a new feature, improve the design, or build new capabilities? This is your opportunity to be creative and make an impact!

Our GitHub repository is open for contributions. Whether you're learning web development, want to practice your coding skills, or have ideas for new features, we welcome your contributions. This is a safe place to learn, experiment, and grow your skills while helping the cloud security community.

About CSOH

Cloud Security Office Hours began in February 2023 as a weekly gathering for cloud security professionals. We're a vendor-neutral community focused on sharing knowledge, learning together, and advancing cloud security practices.

What We Offer

  • Weekly Zoom Sessions: Every Friday, featuring presentations and open discussions
  • Curated Resources: 200+ vetted cloud security resources including labs, tools, training, and career development
  • Community Support: Email us at admin@csoh.org or join our Friday Zoom sessions for discussions and networking
  • Vendor Neutral: We maintain independence and focus on what works best
  • 100% Free: All resources and sessions are freely available to the community

What We Ask

  • Show Up & Participate: Join the Friday Zoom sessions when you can, ask questions, and share your perspective — the community is only as good as the people in it
  • Share Knowledge: Bring a topic, present your research, or recommend a resource that helped you — everyone has something worth teaching
  • Contribute Back: Suggest a new resource, add a news source, fix a typo, or improve the site — no coding required, all skill levels welcome
  • Keep It Vendor-Neutral: Focus discussions on practices, tradeoffs, and what actually works — not sales pitches
  • Be Respectful & Inclusive: Treat members with kindness regardless of experience level; this is a safe place to learn, ask questions, and grow
  • Spread the Word: We don't do paid marketing — if CSOH has helped you, tell a colleague or share a resource with someone who'd benefit
  • Build Your Network: Take advantage of this opportunity to meet peers, swap contacts, and grow your professional circle in cloud security
  • Ask Questions: Don't keep questions to yourself — there are no dumb questions, and someone else is almost certainly wondering the same thing
