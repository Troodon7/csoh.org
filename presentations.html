Community Contributions

Want to Present?

We welcome presentations from community members on cloud security topics. If you have expertise to share or an interesting project to showcase, we'd love to have you present at a future session.

Topics we're interested in include:

  • Cloud security tools and techniques
  • Real-world incident response case studies
  • New vulnerabilities and attack vectors
  • Security automation and DevSecOps
  • Compliance and governance frameworks
  • Career development and certifications

Email admin@csoh.org or bring it up during a Friday Zoom session to propose a presentation.
